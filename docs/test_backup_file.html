Merit-30

Free EAMCET/IIT Coaching Center for government school(ZPHS) merit students from 8th to 12th

Mission:

Merit-30 Mission is to achieve EAMCET/IIT ranks for the merit students from govt schools(ZPHS), by proving free quality coaching from 9th-12th. This can result two things:
  1. Coaching merit students in ZPHS for EAMCET/IIT to enter in to top quality colleges at the free of cost.
  2. By doing the above transforming the BPL families who remain as beneficiaries of the govt subsidies/schools to a dignified tax paying civilians through EAMCET/IIT ranks.

Free EAMCET/IIT Coaching for 9th-12th class merit students:

  • Free Coaching is provided for EAMCET/IIT:Maths, Physics, and Chemistry coaching is provided as part of the program.
  • Free Transport for those who are far away from coaching center, and also one day meal will be provided
  • Admission: Only Govt ZPHS school students will be provided admission from class-8 to class-10.
  • Timings: Working-days: after school from 4.30 to 7.30pm, Holidays: 10.00am to 5.00pm.
  • Free Boys Hostel: To efficiently use the time and to spend most of the student in the revision, Hostel is provided for Boys.
