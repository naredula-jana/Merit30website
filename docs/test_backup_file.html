Merit-30

Free EAMCET/IIT Coaching Center for government school(ZPHS) merit students from 9th to 12th

Mission Statement:

“Education for all is the highest form of service”. We believe in this and are committed to transforming the lives of BPL families from being beneficiaries of government subsidies to becoming dignified, tax-paying citizens. Our mission is to coach meritorious students from ZPHS (govt schools) for EAMCET/IIT exams, providing them with the opportunity to gain admission into top-quality colleges at no cost. By empowering students to achieve academic excellence and secure better job opportunities, we aim to uplift communities and provide a brighter future.

Activities:

  1. Free Coaching for 9th and 10th classes: Merit30 provides Coaching for 9th and 10th after the school hours. Currently around 60 students from the following 5 ZPHS schools are attending 9/10th class EAMCET/IIT coaching:
    • Nizampet ZPHS school( distance=1.8km): Nizampet ZPHS to Center.
    • Hydernagar ZPHS school( distance=3km): Hydernagar ZPHS to Center.
    • Bachpally ZPHS school ( distance=5.6km) Bachupally ZPHS school to center
    • Shamshiguda ZPHS school (distance=4.7km) Shamsiguda ZPHS school
    • Miyapur ZPHS school:(distance=4.3km) Miyapur ZPHS school
  2. Full time Free College for 11th and 12th and Integrated coaching for EAMCET/IIT: We will be starting this activity from the next year onwards(2025-26). Currently there are two batches in 10th class for IIT and EAMCET batches, These two batches will be eligible for the next year 11th class.
  3. Free Boys Hostel for Merit students preparing IIT: Currently supported only for IIT batch, in the future this may be expanded for the EAMCET Batch also.

Output from the above Activities:

By selecting and coaching meritorious students from multiple Zilla Parishad High Schools (ZPHS) and providing them with quality coaching for EAMCET: we aim to achieve two things:
  1. Getting good ranks for EAMCET/IIT . By doing this, we will be uplifting the BPL families of these children, with this we will be transforming the lives of BPL families from being beneficiaries of government subsidies to becoming dignified, tax-paying citizens.
  2. Currently the number of ranks from EAMCET/IIT are very low from ZPHS schools, by doing the above it motivates junior batches to get better EAMCET/IIT , and this will create a ripple effect among large childrens in large ZPHS schools.

Background

Founder: TejaRevanth Foundation is NGO founded by Janardhana Reddy Naredula in the year 2002 . Merit-30 is a NGO, founded to help education for childrens from the BPL families. Founded Merit-30( under the name TejaRevanth Foundation) in Kurnool on 12 Sep, 2002,our NGO has been committed to providing various educational services to children from BPL families. Here is a brief journey overview:

Phases of Growth of TejaRevanth Foundation(NGO):

  1. Phase 1 (2002-2008, Kurnool): Supported orphan children with shelter, food, and education.
  2. Phase 2 (2008-2022, Bangalore): Extended financial support to government school students and provided tuition fees.
  3. Phase 3 (2022-Present, Hyderabad): Launched free EAMCET/IIT coaching center for ZPHS schools.

Evolution of Our Services over the period of time :

Our mission has always been to provide education to children from BPL families. Over the years, we've evolved and learned from our projects, shifting our approach to maximize our impact. Initially, we provided support alone, including education, academics, and shelter, which limited our reach to a small number of children. Recently, we transformed our model to function as a catalyst, focusing on providing coaching to a larger number of government school students. This new approach strengthens government schools and impacts a broader section of children.
