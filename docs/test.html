Objective:

MERIT-30 is a free coaching center for students studying 8th-10th class in govt schools for EAMCET/Polytechnic entrance test. and also provides free software courses for engineering college students who are interested to help in teaching EAMCET courses. Merit-30 selects top 30 students from 10th class on Merit basis and sponsers college fees to make eduction free from 11th till Engineering for Merit students, this is only for the govt school students.

EAMCET Free Coaching

  • Free Coaching is provided for EAMCET: Maths, Physics and Chemistry coaching is provide to Govt school students for EAMCET Coaching.
  • Admission: Only Govt school students will admitted from class-8 to class-10.

Jobs/GATE(CSE) Free Coaching

  • Free Computer courses are provided to train for Jobs/Gate-CSE. These Engineering students will help to coach Class-8 to Class-12 for EAMCET Coaching.
  • Lecturers: IIT'ian and software engineers from MNC's will provide courses.

SponserShip of college fees

Top 30 students will be picked from class-10, and they will be sponsered for college fees from class-11 to class-16. Sponsers will be directly paying the college fees of merit students directly to the college.

Merit30 Stakeholders

  1. Govt High schoold children from 8th to 10th: They are biggest benefiecieres of the Merit-30 project through EAMCET Free Coaching and also through sponsership if they do well in the 10th class and further. Only ZPHS govt school students from 8th to 10th class are eligable for EAMCET free coaching, weekly 10 hours will spend in teaching Maths, science as part of this programme.
  2. Engineering college Students : They will be benifitted through GATE/Job computer courses. The Engineering students who has benefited in the programme can contribute back by teaching EAMCET classes with guidence.
  3. Merit-30 Board Members: IITians and Experienced Software Engineers form this group. They will be contributing by teaching computer courses at free of cost through onine and offline to the engineering college students.
  4. Permanent Staff: Permanent Teachers, cook, transpor staff and other staff will be participating in day-to-day in free coaching activities.
  5. Sponsers: They will be contributing by paying the college fees directly to colleges for the top 30 Merit students in 10th and higher classes.

GATE-CSE coaching

  • Admission: Any Engineering college students
  • Courses: OS, Database, Computer Networks, Data structures, Computer organization, Algorithms, Theory of computation, Compiler design, Digital Logic
Section Topics
Section 1: Engineering Mathematics Discrete Mathematics: Propositional and first order logic. Sets, relations, functions, partial orders and lattices. Monoids, Groups. Graphs: connectivity, matching, coloring. Combinatorics: counting, recurrence relations, generating functions. Linear Algebra: Matrices, determinants, system of linear equations, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, LU decomposition Calculus: Limits, continuity and differentiability. Maxima and minima. Mean value theorem. Integration. Probability: Random variables. Uniform, normal, exponential, poisson and binomial distributions. Mean, median, mode and standard deviation. Conditional probability and Bayes theorem.
Section 2: Digital Logic Boolean algebra. Combinational and sequential circuits. Minimization. Number representations and computer arithmetic (fixed and floating point).
Section 3: Computer Organization and Architecture Machine instructions and addressing modes. ALU, data-path and control unit. Instruction pipelining,pipeline hazards. Memory hierarchy: cache, main memory and secondary storage; I/O interface (interrupt and DMA mode).
Section 4: Programming and Data Structures Programming in C. Recursion. Arrays, stacks, queues, linked lists, trees, binary search trees, binary heaps, graphs.
Section 5: Algorithms Searching, sorting, hashing. Asymptotic worst case time and space complexity. Algorithm design techniques: greedy, dynamic programming and divide-and-conquer. Graph traversals, minimum spanning trees, shortest paths
Section 6: Theory of Computation Regular expressions and finite automata. Context-free grammars and push-down automata. Regular and contex-free languages, pumping lemma. Turing machines and undecidability. .
Section 7: Compiler Design Lexical analysis, parsing, syntax-directed translation. Runtime environments. Intermediate code generation. Local optimisation, Data flow analyses: constant propagation, liveness analysis, common subexpression elimination.
Section 8: Operating System System calls, processes, threads, inter-process communication, concurrency and synchronization. Deadlock. CPU and I/O scheduling. Memory management and virtual memory. File systems.
Section 9: Databases ER-model. Relational model: relational algebra, tuple calculus, SQL. Integrity constraints, normal forms. File organization, indexing (e.g., B and B+ trees). Transactions and concurrency control.
Section 10: Computer Networks Concept of layering: OSI and TCP/IP Protocol Stacks; Basics of packet, circuit and virtual circuit switching; Data link layer: framing, error detection, Medium Access Control, Ethernet bridging; Routing protocols: shortest path, flooding, distance vector and link state routing; Fragmentation and IP addressing, IPv4, CIDR notation, Basics of IP support protocols (ARP, DHCP, ICMP), Network Address Translation (NAT); Transport layer: flow control and congestion control, UDP, TCP, sockets; Application layer protocols: DNS, SMTP, HTTP, FTP, Email.

EMCET coaching

  • Admission: only for govt schools children from 8th to 12th
  • Courses: Maths and Science
  • Timings: 12 hours a week, 4 days a week, every day from 4.30 to 7.30pm
  • Pre-Merit-30 Batch: Free coaching and study material is provided for 8th-10th classes. 9 hours of coaching for Maths & Science subjects with regular exams are conducted per week. Students are admitted from any Govt. and free schools with a screening test. 600 students will be enrolled for the pre-Merit-30 batch.
  • Merit-30 Batch: Free accommodation, food, and full time coaching is provided for 11th-12th classes. Complete care will be given on all days throughout the year. Top 30 students from Pre-Merit-30 batches will be selected through the entrance exam in the 10th standard. 30 students will be enrolled for this batch for 11th class and 30 students for 12th class.
