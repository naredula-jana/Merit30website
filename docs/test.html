Objective:

MERIT-30 is a free coaching center for students studying 8th-10th class strictly from govt schools for EAMCET entrance test. Merit-30 also provide free computer curses Job seekers or those preparing for GATE(CSE) enterance test.

EAMCET Coaching

  • Free Coaching is provided for EAMCET: Maths and Science coaching is provide to Govt school students for Emcet/IIT.
  • Admission: Only Govt school students will admitted from class-8 to class-10.

Jobs/GATE(CSE)

  • Free Computer courses are provided to train for Jobs/Gate-CSE. These Engineering students will help to coach Class-8 to 12 for Emcet Coaching.
  • Lecturers: IIT'ian and software engineers from MNC's will provide courses.

SponserShip for class 11- class 16:

Top 30 students will be picked from class-10, and they will be sponsered for college fees from class-11 to class-16.

Teaching

  • Job/GATE(CSE) Coaching: IITian and experianced Software eningeers will be teaching these courses
  • EAMCET Coaching: Those who are taking Job/GATE coaching will be helping in teaching the EAMCET

Job Coaching

  • Courses: C, C++, Java , Python
  • Artificial Intellegence Courses: AI and Datascience
  • DevOP Courses: AWS

GATE-CSE coaching

  • Admission: Any Engineering college students
  • Courses: OS, Database, Computer Networks, Data structures, Computer organization, Algorithms, Theory of computation, Compiler design, Digital Logic

EMCET coaching

  • Admission: only for govt schools children from 8th to 12th
  • Courses: Maths and Science
